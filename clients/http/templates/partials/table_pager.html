{# Reusable pagination footer for client-side paginated tables. Expects the surrounding wrapper to have `data-paginate-wrap` and at least one descendant with `data-paginate` (a or any container whose direct element children are the paginated rows). The companion JS (`wireTablePagination` in app.js) keeps `data-pager-info` and the row visibility in sync; on a single page the pager hides itself. #}
